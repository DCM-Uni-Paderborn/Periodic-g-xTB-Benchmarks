## Production contract

- native primitive-cell Bloch sampling;
- SPGLIB irreducible-mesh reduction with exact internal full-mesh
  reconstruction and adjoint foldback;
- `save_tblite` full-Fock DIIS;
- `TBLITE/ACCURACY 0.1`, `EPS_SCF 1e-9`, at most 300 SCF iterations;
- no density or DIIS-history reuse between k-point meshes;
- odd meshes unshifted and even MacDonald meshes shifted according to the
  frozen input set.
